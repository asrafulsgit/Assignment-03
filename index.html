Storage Tips for Vegetables

Potatoes

Store potatoes in a cool, dark, and dry place. Avoid storing them near onions, as they can cause potatoes to spoil faster.

Tomatoes

Store tomatoes at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. Do not refrigerate them, as it can affect their flavor and texture.

Onions

Store onions in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated place. Keep them away from potatoes, as the moisture from potatoes can cause onions to spoil faster.
